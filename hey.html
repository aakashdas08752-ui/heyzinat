I don’t know if you still remember me or not, although I’m probably not an important person to be remembered. I can’t understand why I’m still unable to move on from you. Maybe I never even tried…

I’m not messaging you to say that I miss you — although I do — but that’s not the main reason I wanted to reach out. There are a couple of things I’ve wanted to say to you but never did. Believe me, I have to say them before it’s too late.

I feel like I’ve been carrying something with me for so long, and now it’s getting harder to bear. Sometimes people have to let go of their most precious things just to be normal again, and maybe it’s my time now.

Zinat, maybe you hate me, or maybe you’re thinking, “This person is back in my chat list again.” Maybe I’m spoiling your mood on this special day, but I have to be real with you.

It’s been three years since we last talked, and I’ve had so many things to say but never had the courage to contact you.

I’m extremely sorry for what happened three years ago on that picnic. I feel guilty for it. Believe me or not, I was unaware of what they had planned; I was not a part of it. I cried later that night when I found out they had confessed my feelings to you. I knew it was over and that our friendship wouldn’t last long, so I cried very hard. I didn’t cry because you wouldn’t be mine. I cried because I knew I would lose you as a very important part of me.

I never wanted you desperately — I just needed you for the rest of my life as a friend, a mentor, or anything. I just wanted to stay by your side.

You know, I left Kolkata because I didn’t want to make you uncomfortable. I know what type of person you are, and I knew you would feel uncomfortable after seeing me, so I left quietly.

I’ve never stuck to something for more than a year, but it’s been three years now, and you still live in my head rent-free. Pay the rent 😹

Jokes apart, it’s getting very hard for me to live like this. I’m not blaming you; it’s totally on me. The only thing is that I had too many things to say, but the time never came — and that’s why those things are killing me.

1. I want to say sorry.
2. I want to say thank you.
3. Sorry for what I did to you. I understand the situation and I feel truly sorry, but believe me, I had no intentions of hurting you.
4. Thank you, because you’re the person because of whom I’m living a better life. You used to tell me to quit smoking and drinking, but I never listened. When I lost you, I quit. People don’t know the worth of something until they lose it. I have an aim now just because of you. You’re my inspiration. Thank you for giving me a better life.

The situation forced me to confess, and I want you to understand that I wasn’t in my senses that day—I had been drinking with Rajib and Apon, and I was so drunk that I lost control of my emotions. You were on my mind while I was drinking, and suddenly they started talking about you, asking me about you, teasing me by calling your name. In that moment, I broke down in tears and told them everything about you—my feelings for you.

I knew this confession would end everything—and it did. Believe me, I never intended to tell anyone about you, but the situation made me.

I’m extremely sorry if I’ve ruined your day.
